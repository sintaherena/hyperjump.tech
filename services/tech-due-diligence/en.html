Tech Due Diligence

Tech Due Diligence is a structured evaluation of an organization's technology landscape. Designed to uncover hidden risks, assess readiness, and provide clarity for informed decisions.

Whether you're investing in a startup, preparing for a merger, or auditing internal capabilities, our TDD service delivers actionable insights to de-risk your tech journey.

Who is it for?

For those who need clarity, confidence, and insights. Before scaling, investing, or making big tech moves.

  • Investors evaluating the health and scalability of a product or platform
  • Founders/CTOs preparing for funding rounds or acquisitions
  • Enterprises assessing internal capabilities or third-party vendors
  • PE/VC Firms seeking deep-dive audits pre/post-investment
